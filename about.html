Who We Are

Taj Rice was founded with one simple mission: to bring premium basmati rice and quality daily groceries to homes across Pakistan — at honest prices, with zero hassle.

We source our basmati directly from the finest rice fields of Punjab — Super Basmati, Sella, Kainat — ensuring every grain meets our strict quality standards before it reaches your table.

Beyond rice, we carry a complete range of commodities, cooking oils, ghee, masala, tea, coffee, dairy, personal care and household products — everything your family needs, in one place.

Ordering is as simple as sending a WhatsApp message. We deliver to your door and you pay cash on delivery. No apps, no sign-ups, no complications.
